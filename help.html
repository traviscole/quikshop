Frequently Asked Questions


What is Quikshop?

Quikshop is an web based app that allows users to get in and out of a store without ever having to wait in line. This is done by scanning an item into the app through our scanner. When you have finished your shopping you simply checkout with your items in your app's cart and show an employee your reciept that will be emailed to you on your way out.

How do I create an account with Quikshop?
On the homepage press create account and enter your information. After you have completed this you will have an account with Quikshop and will be able to enter the site in order to shop quickly.

Can another family member use my account?
Yes. A single Quikshop account is available to anyone in the same family that has been provided your username and password.

What can I do once I have an account with Quikshop?
You will be able to enter the site, will be eligible to recieve coupon recommendations for the items in your cart, be able to ask for help if needed, and be able to modify your billing information on your profile.

How do I add items to my cart?
Simply scan the item you wish to add to your cart and it will be added. If you are unable to scan your item you will need to manually add your item to your cart by entering the product key.

What type of coupons does Quikshop offer?
Quikshop will offer coupons based on what you have in your cart. If an item is found to match an item that has a coupon available then Quikshop will present you the coupon and allow you to use it.

Is my billing information secure?
Yes.

If I do not have a camera on my phone, can I still use this app?
Yes. You can manually select the items from a store's list and put them in your cart.
